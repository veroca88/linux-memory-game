Rules of the game

This is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards. You have two levels, This is your last chance to decide which level you want to play. After this, there is no turning back. You take the blue pill—the story goes easy. You take the red pill—you stay in Wonderland, and the story goes difficult.
